Welcome to Trash Sorter, a web application dedicated to tackling the issue of improper waste disposal. Our goal is to provide a simple and effective solution to help users correctly classify their waste and contribute to a cleaner environment.

Every year, tons of waste end up in landfills, causing environmental harm. Trash Sorter empowers individuals to make informed decisions about how to dispose of their waste properly. By using image recognition technology, we aim to identify the type of trash and guide users on the appropriate disposal methods.

Together, we can make a positive impact on the environment and work towards a more sustainable future. Join us in the journey of responsible waste management!
